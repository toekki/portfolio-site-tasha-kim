SIMPLY STAY ON TASK, FOCUSED & MOTIVATED

Motiv8 Forward is an iOS app for users looking for a simple task app that has the user keeping track of their tasks and shopping lists, it is also a positive motivation tool.

Timer

that uses the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method that breaks down the work into intervals of 25 minutes in length.

Noisics

a selection of white noise and soft background music.
(Noise + Music = Noisics)

Statistics

that keeps track if the user fulfills all their tasks each day. The more the user consistently commits each day to their tasks, the chain forms. The concept is to not break the chain and see how long of a chain can be made. This is known as the Seinfield Strategy. The comedian? Yes, Jerry Seinfield came up with this concept to beat procrastination and to consistently produce quality work.
